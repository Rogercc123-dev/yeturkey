04

FAQ

When does the general ticket sale start?+
The general ticket sale will start soon. Exact dates are announced on the official ticket page.
What are the ticket prices?+
Ticket prices will be published when ticket sales open.
How many tickets can I buy?+
A maximum of 6 tickets per purchase per show is allowed. Orders that exceed the limit may be cancelled.
Where can I buy tickets?+
Tickets are available exclusively through Bugece. Do not buy tickets from unofficial resale platforms.
Is there a ticket limit per person?+
Yes. Each person can purchase a maximum of 6 tickets per show.
When will I receive my tickets?+
After purchasing your ticket you will receive an order confirmation. Tickets will be available for download shortly before the event.
Do I need to print my tickets?+
No. Tickets can be scanned directly from your phone at the entrance.
Are VIP tickets available?+
Yes. Various VIP packages are available including VIP Floor and VIP Lounge.
Can I resell my tickets?+
Tickets can only be resold through official resale partners such as TicketSwap or Tixel.
What time does the concert start?+
Doors open at 18:00. Show begins at 20:00 sharp. Times are subject to change.
