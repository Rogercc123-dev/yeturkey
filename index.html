FAQ

When does the general ticket sale start?+

The general ticket sale will start soon. Exact dates are announced on the official ticket page.

What are the ticket prices?+

Ticket prices will be published when ticket sales open.

How many tickets can I buy?+

A maximum of 6 tickets per purchase per show is allowed. Orders that exceed the limit may be cancelled.

Where can I buy tickets?+

Tickets are available exclusively through the official ticket website. Do not buy tickets from unofficial resale platforms.

Is there a ticket limit per person?+

Yes. Each person can purchase a maximum of 6 tickets per show.

When will I receive my tickets?+

After purchasing your ticket you will receive an order confirmation. Tickets will be available for download shortly before the event.

I haven't received a confirmation email. What should I do?+

Contact the ticket support team and include the event name, the email address used for purchase, and proof of payment.

Can I change the email address on my ticket order?+

Yes. Contact the support team and provide the event name, the original email address, and proof of payment.

Do I need to print my tickets?+

No. Tickets can be scanned directly from your phone at the entrance.

If I bought multiple tickets, can we enter separately?+

Yes. Each ticket has its own QR code and can be sent to different people.

Are VIP tickets available?+

Yes. Various VIP packages will be available. More information will be announced closer to the ticket sale date.

How do I access the order status page?+

Use the "Download your tickets" link in your confirmation email.

Can I resell my tickets?+

Tickets can only be resold through official resale partners such as TicketSwap or Tixel.

How do I buy wheelchair or accessibility tickets?+

Accessible viewing platform tickets are available through the ticket purchase page. Each wheelchair user may bring one companion.

What time does the concert start?+

Exact times for doors and show start will be announced closer to the event.

Is there an age restriction?+

There is no strict minimum age. However, fans under 16 are recommended to attend with an adult.

Can I bring a bag?+

Large bags and backpacks are generally not allowed. Check the venue's rules before attending.

Is the venue accessible for visitors with disabilities?+

Yes. Accessibility facilities are available at the venue.

If the concert sells out, will a waiting list open?+

Yes. A waiting list may be opened through the official ticket page.
