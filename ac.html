DEFINING LIVE
IN TURKEY

Access Opera is Turkey's premier live entertainment company. Founded in Istanbul, we've built an unparalleled reputation for producing landmark stadium shows, large-scale cultural events, and premium live experiences.

From concept to execution, we handle every aspect of production — artist relations, logistics, staging, ticketing, and on-ground operations. Our team brings together decades of experience in the global entertainment industry.

We don't just produce events. We create moments that define a generation.
